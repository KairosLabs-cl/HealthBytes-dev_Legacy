# HealthBytes - Project Overview

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Quality & Security Hardening
**Updated:** 2026-04-13

## Core Value Proposition

**HealthBytes** is a mobile-first e-commerce platform specializing in health and wellness products for people with dietary restrictions (celiac disease, diabetes, food allergies, intolerances).

### Problem Solved

People with dietary restrictions face difficulties:
- Identifying products safe for their consumption
- Verifying ingredients quickly
- Finding alternatives in one place

### Solution Provided

- Curated product catalog with allergen/dietary information
- Smart filters by dietary needs
- Secure checkout with multiple payment methods
- Mobile-first experience
