# MVP

Initial MVP goal:

Help users managing diabetes, dyslipidemia, or arterial hypertension discover relevant food products and understand recommendation confidence.

MVP scope:

- Product catalog with nutrition and provider data.
- Condition-aware search and filters.
- Recommendation states: recommended, compatible, review, insufficient data, excluded.
- Product detail explanations.
- Mixed commerce: direct checkout where available, external redirection where needed.

Not MVP:

- Full clinical recommendation engine.
- Guaranteed medical suitability.
- AI meal plans.
- Computer vision.
- B2B inventory back-office.
