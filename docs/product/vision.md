# Product Vision

HealthBytes centralizes hard-to-find food products for people with dietary needs tied to health conditions.

The first product core is not generic ecommerce. It is trustworthy discovery: helping users find products, understand why they may fit their needs, and distinguish reviewed products from uncertain ones.

Initial pilot:

- Diabetes
- Dyslipidemia
- Arterial hypertension

MVP value:

1. Discovery: users can find relevant products across scattered providers.
2. Trust: users can understand the reasoning behind product recommendations.

HealthBytes gives recommendations based on product data and user-declared needs. It does not replace clinical care or guarantee medical suitability.
