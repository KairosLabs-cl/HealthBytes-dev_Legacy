# Market And Users

Primary user problem:

People with metabolic or cardiovascular dietary needs often cannot find suitable products in ordinary supermarkets. Products are scattered across specialized stores, small providers, importers, and low-visibility channels.

Initial user groups:

- People managing diabetes.
- People managing dyslipidemia.
- People managing arterial hypertension.
- Family members or caregivers buying for those users.

User need:

- Find products faster.
- Compare product data without reading every label manually.
- Know when data is missing or uncertain.
- Buy directly or reach the provider that has the product.
