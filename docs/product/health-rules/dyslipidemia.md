# Dyslipidemia Criteria

Status: draft.

HealthBytes may recommend products for users managing dyslipidemia only when fat-related nutrition data is available.

Required data:

- Total fat.
- Saturated fat.
- Trans fat when available.
- Fiber when available.
- Ingredients.
- Serving size.

Forbidden claims:

- Do not say the product treats cholesterol or triglycerides.
- Do not infer suitability from "light" or similar claims alone.

Preferred language:

- "Recommended according to available fat and ingredient criteria."
- "Review saturated fat and serving size before deciding."
- "Insufficient data to evaluate this product."
