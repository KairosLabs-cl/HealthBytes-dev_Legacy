# Hypertension Criteria

Status: draft.

HealthBytes may recommend products for users managing arterial hypertension only when sodium data is available.

Required data:

- Sodium.
- Serving size.
- Ingredients.
- Preparation instructions when sodium can change after preparation.

Forbidden claims:

- Do not say the product lowers blood pressure.
- Do not say "safe for hypertension".
- Do not infer suitability from a generic wellness claim.

Preferred language:

- "Recommended according to available sodium criteria."
- "Review sodium per serving before deciding."
- "Insufficient data to evaluate this product."
