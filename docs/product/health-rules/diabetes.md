# Diabetes Criteria

Status: draft.

HealthBytes may recommend products for users managing diabetes only when the product has enough nutrition data to support the recommendation.

Required data:

- Total carbohydrates.
- Sugars.
- Serving size.
- Ingredients.
- Sweeteners or sugar alcohols when present.

Forbidden claims:

- Do not say "safe for diabetics".
- Do not imply medical control or treatment.
- Do not infer suitability from marketing labels alone.

Preferred language:

- "Recommended according to available nutrition criteria."
- "Review serving size and carbohydrate content before deciding."
- "Insufficient data to evaluate this product."
